Lasagna

Description

This is my mom's special homemade lasagna recipe with a meaty, made-from-scratch tomato sauce and a deliciously cheesy filling

Serve with a leafy green salad and crusty garlic bread.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Cheeese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the cheese layer
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake the lasagna
